Indemnity App

Video Demo:

The Indemnity App revolutionizes the motor insurance claim process by leveraging advanced AI technologies. With a user-friendly interface built using React, the app allows users to upload images of vehicle damages, which are processed by the YOLOv8 model for accurate damage detection.

Key Features:

  • AI-driven detection of dents and scratches with synthetic dataset training.
  • Damage report generation and repair cost estimation.
  • Integrated storage solution using Clerk, Neon, and cPanel for user data and results.

This app simplifies and automates the insurance claim process, reducing time and effort for users and insurance companies alike.

My Area of Focus: I was tasked with training the AI model, integrating it with the app to generate damage reports and repair cost estimations. I also worked on connecting the model to the app's interface, developed a basic intents-based chatbot for user interaction, and contributed to the panel parts of the app to ensure smooth operation and data handling.

Voice Assist

The VoiceAssist App is an AI-driven voice tutor designed to make learning accessible for individuals with dyslexia and blindness. Using advanced language models, the app provides personalized, interactive voice-based tutoring in science and history, helping users engage with educational content in a more intuitive way.

Key Features:

  • AI-driven voice tutoring for science and history, tailored to the user's learning needs.
  • Personalized learning experience using a large language model (LLM) for better comprehension.
  • Interactive and engaging content that aids individuals with dyslexia and blindness.

The app aims to break down barriers in education, offering a unique and accessible learning tool for those with learning challenges.

My Area of Focus: I was responsible for hyperparameter optimization, working with the team to fine-tune machine learning models and improve their performance for optimal personalized voice tutoring. My efforts helped enhance the model's efficiency and accuracy, contributing to the app's overall effectiveness.
